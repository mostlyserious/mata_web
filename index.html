What is MATA?

MATA is a group of dedicated individuals, entrepreneurs, companies, and investors to whose primary goal is to attract, cultivate and promote technology talent and businesses throughout the region.

How does MATA work?

When you become a member of MATA, you join our tech community—a connection of individuals dedicated to our region’s success through technology. MATA members have access to resources that will help you make, raise and save money. Whether you are looking to start your first technology company, boost your current workforce, or simply add bottom line savings, MATA can deliver results
